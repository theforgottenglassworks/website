The Forgotten Glassworks

The Forgotten Glassworks is a project aimed at giving an online presence to something that has never been digitized (yet). As a matter of fact, Saul's house is located on an American Island, and it is one of the few houses built in that territory. Even getting there by car is not an easy task. Therefore, through digitization, the artworks that Saul has been creating throughout his entire life will have a more relevant presence and will also have the opportunity to be viewed and appreciated by many more users

The digitization project is divided into three steps:


  • Creation of a website working as an art gallery displaying the major artworks of the artist.
  • Creation of a virtual tour allowing users to see the museum as if they were physically there.
  • Creation of 3D models of the major artworks so as to allow users and scholars to lead in-depth analysis of each artworks that underwent the 3-digitization process. The artworks, after the realization of the 3D models, will be displayed in the website, in order to allow a broader dissemination.
