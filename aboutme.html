About

My name is Davide Apolloni, and I am a student of Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge at Bologna's University.

This project was realized for the exam of Intangible Artifacts, Cultural Heritage and Multimedia, held by professor Sofia Pescarin.


In September 2018 I had the opportunity to visit Saul's house (or, better said, museum). Personally, I was very impressed, and seeing myself surrounded by so much art, culture and creativity inspired the creation of this project, which comes approximately one year later.

From now on, Saul's artworks will be digitized and will have a more relevant presence online, given that they can be viewed by more people. Furthermore, this project is aimed at conserving Saul's artworks better, even though in a pure digital form.
